Cross Section View

Corpus Callosum
Allows the left and right brain to communicate.

Limbic System
Regulates emotion and memory.
Also deals with sexual stimulation,
olfactory processes, long-term memory, motivation,
learning, and behavior.

Thalamus
Messenger of motor and sensory signals to the greater cerebral cortex.

Hypothalamus
Controls blood pressure, body temperature, and heart rate.
Also controls the Pituitary Gland and releases hormones.

Amygdala
Responsible for the emotions anger, sadness, and fear. Also responsible for aggression.

Hippocampus
Responsible for long-term memory storage and remembrance.

Reticular Formation/Reticular Activating System
Regulates motivation, consciousness, and arousal.
