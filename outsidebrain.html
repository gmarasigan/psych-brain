Outside View

Frontal Lobe
Controls cognition, such as: thinking, planning, problem solving,
short term memory, language, speech, and movement.

Parietal Lobe
The sensory center of the brain, takes input from the 5 senses
(touch, sight, scent, taste, hearing) and interprets it.

Temporal Lobe
Controls hearing, emotions, understanding language, and memory.

Occipital Lobe
The visual center of the brain,
it registers vision from the eyes and interprets into colors, faces, depth, etc.

Medulla
Controls heartbeat, breathing and blood pressure.

Cerebellum
Responsible for muscle control, such as: balance, and movement.

Broca’s Area
Works to speak words with help from the motor cortex.

Wernicke’s Area
Works to comprehend speech.
